I’m Todor Lazarov Juji — a stuntman, actor, and stunt coordinator from Bulgaria.

You’ve seen my work in Troy, Zero Dark Thirty, and The Expendables 2. I don’t just perform action — I build it, shape it, and execute it with precision.

My craft spans horses, high falls, full burns, sword fighting, and rigging. I thrive where risk meets control. When it comes to fight choreography, there’s no hesitation — only execution.

In Judo and Sambo, I’m a 15-time Bulgarian champion. I’ve stood on the podium at both the World and European Championships, earning 3rd place in each.

This isn’t just what I do.
It’s what I’ve mastered.
